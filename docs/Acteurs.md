# 👥 Acteurs du Système

[[Index|← Back to Index]]

---

## 1. Administrateur (Platform Owner)

The admin is the sole account creator and platform manager.

- Manages user accounts (create, edit, delete, assign roles)
- Manages subjects, departments, classes and academic years
- Configures plagiarism detection threshold (similarity %)
- Manages database backups
- Views global statistics across the platform

---

## 2. Encadrant (Supervisor)

The supervisor oversees assigned projects and reviews student submissions, assisted by the plagiarism system.

- Logs in with an admin-created account (no self-registration)
- Creates and configures PFA subjects (expected deliverables, target classes)
- Reviews student applications (CV + cover letter) and accepts/rejects them
- Consults the plagiarism report for each submission
- Validates or rejects submitted reports, assigns a grade
- Searches the archive of past projects
- Views statistics for their supervised projects

---

## 3. Étudiant (Student)

The student is the main actor of the PFA process.

- Logs in with an admin-created account (no self-registration)
- Browses available PFA subjects
- Applies to a subject by submitting CV + cover letter
- Once accepted: uploads report and source code
- Can modify a submission before supervisor validation
- Tracks their application status (pending / accepted / rejected)
- Views grade and supervisor feedback
- Browses archived past PFAs (based on permissions)
